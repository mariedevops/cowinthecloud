Berta the cow is inexplicably floating on a cloud. She has no idea how she got there. 
You're the player, and your job is to keep her drifting as far as possible without falling off. 
The cloud slowly shrinks over time (raining itself away), winds push her sideways, birds, jets and planes try to knock her off. 
Simple physics. One finger to play.
The world just gets harder the further Berta travels. There's no "Level Complete" screen. The score is the journey. 
This makes it infinitely replayable and easy to share ("I got 4,200 meters, beat that").
